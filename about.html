About Me

A little about me


I am from Belize, a country located in the Caribbean. English is the official language of Belize, but most of the population also speaks Creole, and many Belizeans are multilingual. I am Mestizo, which means I am of mixed Spanish and Maya descent. The Spanish comes from my mother's Mexican heritage, and the Mayan comes from my Father's Indigenous background. In fact, Maya are Belize's indigenous peoples, and are the direct descendants of the original Indigenous inhabitants of the Yucatán peninsula.
